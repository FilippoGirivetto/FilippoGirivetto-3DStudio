About Me

I'm a professional 3D artist creating stunning visual content for games, films, and advertising. My passion lies in bringing creative visions to life through detailed 3D modeling, texturing, and rendering.

I specialize in character design, environment creation, and product visualization, with expertise in industry-standard software including Blender, Maya, Substance Painter, and Unreal Engine.

My work has been featured in international exhibitions and I've collaborated with major studios to create immersive digital experiences.
