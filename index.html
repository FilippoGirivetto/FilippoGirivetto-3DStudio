Hi, I’m Filippo

I'm a 3D artist blending traditional visual storytelling with cutting-edge technology. I specialize in creating immersive experiences through static renders, motion graphics, and AI-generated imagery and video. Welcome to my world of digital experimentation.

About Me

I've always been passionate about sketching, photography, cinema, and especially CGI, constantly developing new workflows that integrate artificial intelligence into the creative process.

I’ve worked with a design and architectural studio, producing event pre-visualizations, motion graphics, and video editing for large firms. My work is driven by visual storytelling and a desire to explore the boundaries between digital and physical reality.
