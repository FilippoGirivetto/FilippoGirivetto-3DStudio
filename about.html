About Me

I've always been passionate about visual arts — from sketching to photography and cinema. My journey in 3D began as a self-taught artist exploring Blender. I bring a deep curiosity and commitment to mastering my craft.

I’ve collaborated with a design and architectural studio creating event pre-visualizations, motion graphics, and video edits for major firms. These experiences have shaped a professional, collaborative mindset.

I also explore ways to integrate the latest AI technologies into my creative workflow, adding new layers to how I approach visual storytelling.
